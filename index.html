Introducing an extensible editor

Blogr feature an exceedinly intuitive interface which let you focus on one thing: creating content. The editor supports management of multiple blogs and allow easy manipulation of embeds such as images, videos and Markdowns. Extensibility with plugins and themes provides easy way to add functionality or change the look of blog.

Robust content management

Flexible content management enables users to easilt move through posts, increase the usability of your blog by addind customized categories, sections, format of flow. With this functionality, you are in full control.

State of the Art Infrastructure

With reliability and speed in mind, worldwide data centers provides the backbone for ultra-fast connectivity. This ensures your site will load instantly, no matter where you readers are, keeping your site competitive.

Free, Open, Simple

Blogr is an open source application backed by a large community of helpul developers. it supports features such as code syntax highlighting, RSS feeds, social media integration, third-party commenting tools, and works seamlessly with google analytics. the architecture is clean and relatively easy to learn.

Powerful Tooling

Batteries included, we built a simple and straight forward CLI tools that make customization and deployment a breeze, but capable of producing even the most complicated of sites.
